Galux Hotel Marketing Campaign

A comprehensive marketing strategy for a three-star hotel in Islamabad

About the Project

Galux Hotel, located in the heart of G-11, Islamabad, needed a comprehensive marketing strategy to increase bookings and brand awareness. The campaign focused on positioning the hotel as a comfortable, affordable home away from home, particularly targeting seasonal travelers from Gilgit during winter months.

Strategy & Implementation

The campaign utilized a multi-channel approach, combining digital marketing, influencer partnerships, and traditional advertising methods. Key components included:

  • • Social media marketing on Instagram and WhatsApp Business
  • • Influencer partnerships with travel vloggers
  • • Seasonal promotional discounts
  • • Strategic partnerships with local travel services
  • • Comprehensive brand identity development

Brand Identity

Developed a cohesive brand identity with:

  • • Custom logo design featuring a crown-shaped building
  • • Brand color palette using bottle green and gold
  • • Tagline: "Feel at Home"
  • • Slogan: "Comfort which makes you forget home"

Marketing Materials

Created comprehensive marketing materials including:

  • • Digital and print brochures
  • • Social media content calendar
  • • Promotional posters and standees
  • • TV commercials and slideshows
  • • Email marketing templates

Results & Impact

The campaign successfully positioned Galux Hotel as a preferred accommodation choice in Islamabad, particularly for winter travelers. The combination of strategic digital marketing, influencer partnerships, and traditional advertising methods led to increased brand awareness and direct bookings.
